Whether you are working on the physical build or the code that runs it, we use a template system to keep contributions organized.

### Hardware Designs
A core objective of OpenRefill is to bring the cost of the dispenser below **$35**. Can you help us achieve this? 
* **Bug Fixes:** Found an issue with the BOM or a CAD file? Submit a report using our [Hardware Bug Fix Template](#link).
* **Enhancements:** Have a design improvement? Propose it using our [Hardware Enhancement Template](#link).
* **Show & Tell:** Made something cool? Customized your housing? Post it to our [Show and Tell Discussion Page](#link).

### Firmware
Help us keep the dispenser running smoothly and efficiently.
* **Bug Fixes:** Found a glitch in the code? Submit an issue using our [Firmware Bug Fix Template](#link).
* **Enhancements:** Wrote a cleaner function or a new feature? Propose it using our [Firmware Enhancement Template](#link).

## ğŸ’¡ Ideas & Feature Requests

Got an idea on how to bring the hardware cost down? Or an enhancement to improve the durability or sustainability of the unit? Any other ideas are also highly welcome.

**Our Process:**
1. **Discuss:** Start by posting your concept on our [Ideas Discussion Page](#link). This allows the community to brainstorm, refine, and validate the idea.
2. **Propose:** Once the idea has gained traction and a clear direction, you or a maintainer can formalize it by submitting an issue using our [Feature Request Template](#link).

### Help the Community (Q&A)
One of the most valuable ways to contribute is by helping others. 
* Visit our [Q&A Discussion Page](#link). 
* If you see a question that you can answer, please jump in! Sharing your specific use cases or "lessons learned" helps build our collective knowledge base.

### Build the Community in Your Country
Rally the troops and spread the word! We are actively looking for local knowledge regarding:
* Gathering and reporting on local **labeling requirements**.
* Gathering and reporting on local **metrology (measurement) requirements**.

If you can provide insights for your region, please start a thread in your country page. 
Don't see your country? Please contact a team member, moderator or email us at hello@openrefill.com.
